About

Learn More About Us

Only a stone's throw away from Salthill beaches, the Anno Santo Hotel has been welcoming visitors to Galway for over 60 years.

We are a family run hotel, where 3 generations have offered guests from all over the world a friendly Galway welcome for over 60 years. Guests are welcome to relax in front of the stove and enjoy the hotel's cafe, which serves tea and coffee until late.

  • Greeted with a welcoming smile.
  • Tips on what to see in the city.
  • Clean and comfortable rooms.

Minutes from Galway’s bustling medieval city centre, the coastal resort of Salthill and the wild countryside of Connemara, our hotel is a great base from which to enjoy the total Galway experience. Salthill is a great choice for travellers interested in scenery, walking and sightseeing.
